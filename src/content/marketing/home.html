Men’s Better Sweater® Fleece Vest

★★★★★ 4.8 from 516 reviews
$129

The Better Sweater® Vest earns the hook by the door. Its 10-oz recycled polyester fleece brings warmth to the core, while the sleeveless cut leaves the arms free and the full-length zip makes it easy to adjust on the move. The inside feels soft and familiar; the outside has a clean sweater-knit face. A regular fit leaves comfortable room for a tee, base layer or button-down and fits neatly beneath a shell.

With a pack on, the construction starts to matter. Offset shoulder seams sit away from the straps, flat seams reduce bulk and help prevent chafing, and brushed-tricot trim keeps the armholes and hem soft against the layers below. The zip-through stand collar adds coverage at the neck. Two zippered handwarmer pockets and a vertical chest pocket keep a phone, keys and small trail essentials secure.

The body fleece is 100% recycled polyester, dyed with a lower-impact heather process that uses less dyestuff, energy and water than conventional methods. The fabrics are bluesign® approved. The vest is made in a Fair Trade Certified™ factory, which means the people who made it earned a premium for their labor. Machine-washable care keeps things simple, and a sweater stone helps extend the useful life of the knit.

At 394 g, it brings useful warmth to a cold start and remains easy to carry through the rest of the day. Over a base layer, it takes the edge off at the trailhead. The full-length zip opens as the first miles warm up, and a shell fits over it when the weather turns. The pack comes off, but the vest can stay on; the sweater-knit face carries easily through the drive home or over a button-down the next morning. It is the kind of layer that spends more time in use than in the wardrobe.

Buyer questions answered
What makes it feel distinctly Patagonia?

Its value is quiet and functional: an understated sweater-knit face, pack-ready construction and environmental choices supported by specific material and factory standards.

Is it comfortable with a backpack?

Yes. Offset shoulder seams are designed for comfort under pack straps, and flat seams reduce bulk and chafe.

How does it layer?

The regular fit works comfortably over a tee, base layer or button-down, while the sleeveless cut keeps arms free.

What can I carry securely?

Two zip handwarmer pockets and one vertical zip chest pocket keep a phone, keys and small essentials close.

What makes the materials more considered?

The fleece and trim use 100% recycled polyester; the dye process reduces dyestuff, energy and water use, fabrics are bluesign® approved, and the vest is made in a Fair Trade Certified™ factory.

How do I keep it looking good?

Machine wash warm and tumble dry low. A sweater stone can refresh the knit’s surface and help extend its useful life.
